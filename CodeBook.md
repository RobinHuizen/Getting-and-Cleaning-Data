# CodeBook

## General explanation of variable names
- **Time**: Measurements in the time domain (directly measured)
- **Frequency**: Measurements in the frequency domain (after Fourier transform)
- **Mean**: Mean value
- **Std**: Standard deviation
- **Jerk**: Derivative of acceleration (rate of change of acceleration)
- **Magnitude**: Magnitude of the vector (combined from X, Y, and Z axes)
- **X, Y, Z**: Directions/axes of the measurement
